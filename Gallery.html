Hajj Traning Program 2024

Quran O Sunnah Foundation is pleased to announce the successful conclusion of the Hajj Training Program 2024. This program was meticulously organized to prepare Hajj pilgrims for their sacred journey, ensuring they were well-equipped with the necessary knowledge and practical skills.

Dora Tarjuma Quran 2024

Quran O Sunnah Foundation had the honor of hosting a special program: Taraweeh with Translation. This initiative aimed to enhance the spiritual experience of Ramadan by providing a deeper understanding of the Quranic recitations during Taraweeh prayers through detailed translations.

Free Palastin Program

Quran O Sunnah Foundation (QSF) recently organized a significant program aimed at advocating for Palestinian freedom and promoting the boycott of Israeli products. This impactful event brought together various political party members, scholars, and community leaders to address these pressing issues and unite voices in support of justice and peace.

QC1 High Court Bar Association & Certificate Distribution Ceremony

Quran O Sunnah Foundation (QSF) proudly organized Quran Course 1 at the High Court Bar Association, aiming to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of the Quran. The course culminated in a meaningful Certificate Distribution Ceremony, celebrating the achievements of the participants.

Quran Courses QC1 to QC7

Quran O Sunnah Foundation (QSF) is proud to offer a series of Quranic education programs, ranging from Quran Course 1 (QC1) to Quran Course 7 (QC7). Each course is meticulously designed to cater to different levels of Quranic study, providing comprehensive learning opportunities for participants of all ages.

Salah Cycle Project at Galaxy Model School

Galaxy Model School is honored to collaborate with Quran O Sunnah Foundation to present the Salah Cycle Project. This impactful initiative is designed to encourage and motivate students to uphold the practice of performing daily prayers consistently. It reflects our joint commitment to nurturing the spiritual development and religious discipline of our students.
